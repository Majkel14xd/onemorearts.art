MONICA GROBELNA
PHOTOGRAPHER, SHE WAS BORN IN WROCŁAW, SHE HAS BEEN TAKING
PHOTOS FOR 25 YEARS – MAINLY THE ARCHITECTURE OF WROCŁAW
CITY BUILDINGS, NOOKS AND CRANNIES IN WROCŁAW THAT ARE NOT ALWAYS
ACCESSIBLE, INTERESTING INTERIORS
AS MONICA SAYS: “I LOVE MY CITY AND I LOVE PHOTOGRAPHING IT”
SHE ALSO CREATES ARTISTIC PORTRAITS
SHE HAS THREE ANALOG LENSES WITH WHICH SHE: “I PAINT PICTURES –
ESPECIALLY NATURE”
FOR MONICA PHOTOGRAPHY IS PRIMARILY AN ART OF OBSERVATION
A MIXTURE OF SENSITIVITY, CREATIVITY AND INSPIRATION
IT IS A COMPOSITION OF COLORS, SHAPES, LIGHT AND WHEN CREATING A
PAINTING, SHE TRIES TO TAKE THESE ELEMENTS INTO ACCOUNT
HER PHOTOGRAPHS DEPICT EVERYDAY LIFE
SHE HAS AN EASY OF VISION, PERCEPTION OF THE WORLD AND THINGS THAT
HELP HER IN PHOTOGRAPHY
PHOTOGRAPHY IS A PLEASURE AND FREEDOM FOR HER, IT’S LIKE A STRESS
RELIEVER, SHE ENJOYS IT
MONICA SAYS: “SOMETIMES I BREAK THE PHOTOGRAPHY RULES TO GET A
MORE INTERESTING EFFECT AND AT THE SAME TIME I PAY GREAT ATTENTION
TO THE AESTHETICS OF THE PHOTO
