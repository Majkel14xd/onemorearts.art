COLLAGE ARTIST, VISUAL ARTIST, GRADUATE OF POSTGRADUATE STUDIES IN ARTISTIC DISCIPLINES IN ARCHITECTURE AT THE ACADEMY OF FINE ARTS
THE ARTIST CREATES IN THE COLLAGE TECHNIQUE
GIVES IMAGES A SPECIFIC, QUIVERING CHARACTER
SHE DOES NOT USE PAINTS IN HER ART, SHE REPLACES THEM WITH PAPER MADE OF COLORED NEWSPAPERS, WHICH SHE FIXES WITH A GEL MEDIUM
EACH MAGDALENA PAINTING IS MADE OF HUNDREDS OF SMALL PIECES OF PAPER
IN HER ARTWORKS MAGDA PRESENTS LANDSCAPES, ESPECIALLY URBAN ONES
HER COLLAGES REFLECT THE ATMOSPHERE OF THE PLACE, THE MOOD OF THE REASON, THE TIME OF DAY
THE IMAGES ARE MULTICOLORED, ENERGETIC AND AT THE SAME TIME INANIMATE
THE ARTIST IN HER COLLAGE TECHNIQUE AND EXPLORATION, ARCHIEVES A THREE-DIMENSIONAL EFFECT, USING THE PLAY OF CHIAROSCURO
MAGDALENA’S ARTWORKS ARE IN PRIVATE COLLECTIONS IN POLAND AND ABROAD
